Fragments · Twelve years

A thousand small mornings.

Found moments from the campus — shot between lessons, meals and the long walks home.

Reel 01 / Education

Patiently. Slowly. Completely.

Each child has an individualised learning plan — speech therapy, occupational therapy, life skills — taught by our divine teachers.

Reel 02 / Nutrition

Food as medicine, as ritual.

Three meals a day, prepared on-site with seasonal ingredients. Mothers, children and teachers eat at the same long table.

Reel 03 / Health

A clinic that comes to the village.

Every month, visiting doctors, dentists and therapists set up a free health camp. No charge. No queue. No questions.

Reel 04 / Presence

Always at the gate.

The Chairman, the teachers and the volunteers live with the children. The work is not a programme. It is a way of being.

The land that holds us

The jungle is our classroom.

Children heal in open skies, under old trees, beside the river. We teach with the land, not against it. Our village is built brick by brick into the soil of Madhupur.

The bell does not ring for anyone to hurry. The lesson ends when the child smiles.
