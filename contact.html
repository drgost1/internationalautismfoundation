§ 04 — Stand with us

Four ways to make a mark.

We do not accept gifts we cannot account for. Choose a path below — our coordinator will guide you, step by step.

Sponsor a child

A modest monthly gift that funds education, meals and medical care for one divine child.

Offer a blessing

A one-time gift — small or large — directed to the work that needs it most that month.

Volunteer

Therapists, teachers, doctors and quiet volunteers are always welcome at the Madhupur campus.

Fund the village

A brick, a classroom, a clinic — contribute to the long-term construction of the Autism Village.
